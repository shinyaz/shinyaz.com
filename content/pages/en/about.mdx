## About Me

Hi, I'm **Shinya Tahara** (shinyaz) -- a Solutions Architect in the Financial Services Industry division at Amazon Web Services Japan. I provide technical guidance to customers, primarily in the financial industry, helping them leverage AWS to build scalable and reliable systems.

Before joining AWS, I spent 10 years at Nomura Research Institute (NRI) as an Infrastructure Engineer, designing, building, and operating mission-critical large-scale systems for the retail and financial/securities industries. Drawing on deep experience across both on-premises and public cloud environments, I now help customers with cloud migration, AI/ML adoption, and modern application development. This blog is where I share technical notes and learnings from my work.

## Areas of Expertise

- **Cloud Architecture**: Scalable system design and technical guidance centered on AWS
- **AI/ML**: Amazon Bedrock, SageMaker, LLM applications, RAG architectures
- **Infrastructure Design & Operations**: Stable operation and modernization of mission-critical systems
- **Container Technologies**: Kubernetes, OpenShift, Docker
- **Automation**: Ansible, Terraform, CI/CD pipelines
- **Security**: PCI-DSS compliant system design and operations
